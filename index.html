Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a bus ticket on Yathra?

To book a ticket, enter your travel details (From, To, and Date) in the search section on the homepage. Select your preferred bus, choose your seat, and complete the booking by making a payment.

How do I track my bus in real-time?

After booking, you will receive a tracking link via SMS or email. Click on the link to view your bus's live location and estimated arrival time.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes, cancellations and rescheduling are allowed, subject to the bus operator’s policies. Check your ticket confirmation email for details or log in to your account to manage your booking.
